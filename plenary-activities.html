LEGISLATIVE ACTIVITIES

SUPPORTING A MOTION

Supporting the motion on the need to support Local manufacturing. I pointed out Innoson as a reference point, we as a government need to support them, firstly by using their vehicles as our official cars.

SPEAKING IN SUPPORT OF A MOTION

Contributing to a motion on the International day of poverty Eradication.

SUPPORTING A MOTION

Seeking the house to support a motion regarding the decision by Nigerian Ports Authority to terminate its contract with Intels.

SPEAKING IN SUPPORT OF A MOTION

Speaking in support of a motion raised by my Honourable colleague on commercialization of some services of the Nigerian Army.

SUPPORTING A MOTION

Supporting the motion raised by Hon. Chinda on the breach of Section 92 of the constitution by INEC.

MOTION ON THE MISUSE OF EXPORT EXPANSION GRANT

Speaking in support of a motion on the misuse of Export Expansion Grant.

URGENT NEED TO INVESTIGATE THE REDUCTION OF CUT-OFF MARK

Speaking on the urgent need to investigate the reduction of cut of mark by the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board. I believe lowering the cut-off will lower the standard of education in tertiary institutions

AN ACT TO AMEND THE COASTAL AND INLAND SHIPPING CABOTAGE ACT.

Moving that the Bill for an Act to amend the coastal and inland shipping cabotage act No. 5 of 2005, to restrict the use of foreign vessels in domestic coastal trade be read for the second time.

SUPPORTING A BILL

Supporting a bill to encourage the training of Officers of the Nigerian Airforce domestically.

ROSE INSUPPORT OF A BILL

Supporting a bill as raised by my Honourable colleague.

On the need to invite the Military Chiefs regarding the deployment of the Military to State Assembly

FIELDING QUESTIONS

Fielding a question to the Honourable Minister of Industry, trade and investment on the dumping of goods on the country.

Supporting a motion

Speaking in support of the motion to tap from the pension fund while the funds sit idle.
