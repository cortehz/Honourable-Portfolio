My Ideology

I believe that as Public Servants, we owe it to the people to actually serve, to bring to the fore, the interest of the people and leave behind every selfish or parochial agenda. To represent, to lead and also to follow. Follow the people so as to know their needs, put their shoes on to know exactly where it hurts.

The people of Ado/Ogbadibo/Okpokwu are my top most priority at every point. They are the reason I'm at the Lower Chambers of the National Assembly, the reason I deem fit to stand up and be heard. I intend to continue in such realm.

Our greatest joy as public servants should lie in the fact that our constituents lack not the most basic of amenities, like pipe borne water, electricity, food, access to quality education. If as representatives, we can start by making sure these basic needs are first met, the Nigeria of our dreams is well on it's way to being built.

It is my intention to be part of Nigeria's success story, to be part of the good bunch that changed the course of our beloved Country.
God bless NIGERIA!!
